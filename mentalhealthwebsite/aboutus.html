About Us

We are a team of researchers and designers studying DT (Design and Technology) and SDM (Strategic Design and Management) at the New School Parsons. We are all passionate about the topic and issue of mental health and mental illness. Our theoretical design studies have allowed us to identify a gap in the way mental health information has previously been approached. We hope this website acts as a safe and authentic space where those struggling, or looking to learn, can find free unbiased helpful information for coping with mental illness as well as quick laughs from some relatable content. We aim to change the dialogue surrounding mental illness and hope this website is able to tackle some of the many stigmas surrounding mental illness. While also shifting the solutions presented away from consumerism and commodification and back towards knowledge and awareness.
